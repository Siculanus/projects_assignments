For my assignment I selected a specific quote from a famous philosopher and chose to add it to “monkeylearn.com” and to “wordclouds.com”.







The first one is the product made by “monkeylearn.com”. The page seems to select only the words it considers to be more important, leaving out the rest of the text. I found the activity in both to be quite limited and uninteresting, but this one was by far more limited, even though the result actually has a better visual. You can change the size of the font, the order and shape of the letters, the colors both from the words and background



The latter is the product done by “wordclouds.com”. The quote appears in its fullness and there is a gigantic variety of shapes that can be used to order the words. I have failed however to find one which would make the quote actually readable. For this reason, I have found the tools to be unable to produce my expected results. Images can be downloaded as images or as a csv file, showing up as a table that tells us how many times words are used.
